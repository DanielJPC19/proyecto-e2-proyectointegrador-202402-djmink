The "Sonic Landscapes" project aims to explore and document the rich cultural diversity of Colombia through music. By combining ethnographic research with modern music production techniques, the project seeks to create a series of original compositions that reflect the unique soundscapes of various Colombian regions. Students will engage in fieldwork, collecting traditional sounds and musical elements from different communities, and then use this material to produce contemporary music that honors and celebrates Colombia's cultural heritage.
